W101: Backpack Item Lock

SUMMARY: Backpack Item Lock is used to "lock" an item and prevent it from being deleted. Backpack Item Lock is NOT enabled by default, and it needs to be enabled via the Wizard101 Spellbook Settings to be utilized within the Player's Backpack. 

Note: Press ESC to open your Spellbook Settings.

  1. Click on Gameplay Options 2.
  2. Click the arrow beside Backpack Item Lock to toggle this option to YES.
  3. Click OK to save the changes.
